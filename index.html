About Nishi

A practitioner
who truly listens

Nishi Bhansali is a Certified Sound Healing Practitioner who brings the ancient wisdom of Himalayan singing bowls, chimes, and vibrational instruments into modern spaces — helping people quiet the noise and come home to themselves.

Her sessions create a deeply immersive soundscape where your body can let go, your nervous system can soften, and your mind finds stillness. Whether one-on-one or in a group, each experience is held with care, intention, and deep presence.

Nishi is available for private sessions, group sound baths, corporate wellness events, and retreats — anywhere in the world.
